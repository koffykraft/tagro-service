How to link R2 Schematics with database records

1. The Image Layer (R2 Public Bucket)

All schematic diagram page images are converted to binary PNG fragments and saved directly into the R2 bucket. Your app accesses them directly at:

https://manuals.tagro.in/stihl/{model_key}/pages/p{page_no}.png
2. The Text Mapping Layer (Cloudflare KV)

The python uploader parses spreadsheet columns to match parts metadata to specific positions. When the app loads, it queries KV to overlay interactive bounding boxes over R2 image coordinates.

3. Live India June 2026 Prices (Cloudflare KV)

Part numbers are automatically normalized to remove formatting discrepancies and search-matched against the master price index in KV to append the most current, verified Indian Rupee price matrix.
